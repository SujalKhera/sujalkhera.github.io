03Design Principles
01

Function Determines Form

Every geometric decision needs a reason — function, material logic, or manufacturing constraint. If a form cannot justify itself, it is removed. Decoration is a last resort, never a starting point.

02

Hesitation is a Design Failure

If a user pauses to understand how something works, the design has already failed. Affordance, visual hierarchy, and interaction cost are the real material of product design — before any physical material is chosen.

03

Prototype to Kill Ideas Fast

Paper before CAD. CAD before renders. Physical prototypes before presentations. The fastest path to a good idea is building the bad ones first and extracting the lesson from each failure without sentiment.
